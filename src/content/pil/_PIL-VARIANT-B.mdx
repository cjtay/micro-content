# Carboplatin and Docetaxel Protocol

## About the treatment
Carboplatin and docetaxel combination is a standard chemotherapy treatment for non-small cell lung cancer. This platinum-based regimen works by damaging cancer cell DNA and disrupting cell division, effectively slowing or stopping tumor growth.
