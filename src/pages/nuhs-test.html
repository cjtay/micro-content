Care in the Community

At NUHS, we are committed to enhancing the health of the community we serve. By leveraging on our position within the NUHS Academic Health System, we adopt a population health approach that encompasses facilitating quality, cost-effective care, supporting health and wellness in the community, and empowering you to take ownership of your own health.

One-stop Specialist and Ambulatory Centre


Managed by NUHS since January 2021, Jurong Medical Centre (JMC) is a one-stop specialist and ambulatory centre which brings outpatient specialist care and day surgery services right to the doorstep of residents in the west.

A Night to Remember: Coming Together to Celebrate Our Best and Most Dedicated People!
The NUHS Tribute Awards Ceremony is a biennial event where we recognise NUHS staff who have made outstanding contributions to patient care, research, and education in our academic health system. Joining us for the 5th edition of the awards ceremony this year was Deputy Prime Minister & Minister for Trade and Industry, Mr Gan Kim Yong. At the prestigious event, we gave out 67 awards – including the NUHS Pinnacle Awards, Distinguished Senior Clinician Awards, as well as Emeritus Consultant and Fellow Conferments - to 77 outstanding recipients. 

NUHS Caregivers' Day 2025
Graced by Dr Amy Khor, Senior Minister of State for Sustainability and the Environment, and Transport, we honoured 19 caregivers at the annual NUHS Caregiver Appreciation Ceremony, recognising their dedication and vital role in patient care. The event also marked the launch of the Patient and Family Advocacy Council at Tengah General and Community  Hospital (TGCH) inviting patients, caregivers, and residents to shape the upcoming TGCH.
